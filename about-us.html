What we do

Making farming better for every one

We help farmers in starting, operating, and growing their produce, we assist farmers in achieving independence. We are lowering the barriers to farming because we think that more people will have a voice in it in the future, and this will benefit everyone.

Creating farmers platform for impact

we care deeply about the work we do We are constant learners who thrive on change and seek to have an impact in everything we do. The primary goal of UFARM, which was developed by a group of software engineers from Refactory, is to improve the livelihood and family income of farmers in Masajja Division. This platform unites all local farmers, and because they can now sell their produce online, the framers' income has increased significantly.
